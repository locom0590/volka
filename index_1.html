The Story

Three continents, one thread of sound

Photo · Early Years
Video · Journey
Photo · Studio

Twenty‑five years ago, music was simply the thing that made sense when nothing else did. Since then, that thread has run through three continents — conservatories, community halls, and quiet living rooms — each place teaching something the last one couldn't.

What stayed constant was the belief that sound is not a performance, but a relationship. With children, that means meeting them exactly where they are. With adults, it means making space to simply feel again.

Today, that same attention shapes every session — whether it's a first mallet lesson with a four‑year‑old, or a sound bath for a room full of tired professionals.
